Administrative Details

For this project we wanted a real client that could give us some specific needs for the website and also motivate us by having the specific objective to help someone that is in need of a website. As it happens, one of our group members knew someone who previously had created their own website, but wanted an upgrade. We therefore settled on our client, Pål Hembre.

Pål Hembre and his three sons are active in competitive pistol shooting, where all of them have impressive results to show to. Pål has previously created a website that showcases their accomplishments and competition statistics in said sport. However, he has recently suggested that his website is in need of an upgrade and that he is looking for someone to give it a slightly cleaner and professional look. We se this as the perfect opportunity to create a new, functional and impressive website that will actually be of use to our client.

Purpose and goals

We had a short meeting with Pål where we talked about what he wanted to use the website for and how he wanted it to function. During this meeting we outlined our own timeframe and restrictions for the project as to give our client realistic expectations for the final product. Pål explained to us that the purpose of the website is to attract sponsors to further the clients carreers as athletes.

The information and data the client provided can in itself be quite confusing as there are a lot of statistics from various championships and other competitions. Our goal is therefore to structure and prioritize the information so that the accomplishments of the athletes become clear and therefore attract potential sponsors. A description of the athletes in an "about", for example, will also be included to add a more personal feel, and therefore be more convincing.

Audience

The intended audience for this webpage is going to be mainly sponsors but also the general public who are eager to know more about this families shooting ability. For the sponsors it would be necessary to see if the different members of the Hembre family has achieved soething remarkable, like gotten medals or broken records. This can be showed by statistics and also videos. There could be in there interest to also look at what other companies and partners they have to know if they are really proffesional. For the general public the pictures, videos and statistics seem interesting.

The content of the site and how it is organized

The main content of the site will be divided into the main page, each member of the Hembre families own page, sponsors, history and statistics.

  • Main page
    • Navigation bar(consistent on all pages)
    • Videos and pictures
    • News
    • Slideshow
    • Grid showing all 4 Hembre's
    • Footer with contact information(will be on all the sites)
  • Each member of the Hembre families own page
    • Pictures and videos/ links to videos
    • Medals
    • Facts
  • Statistics per person
    • Statistics
    • Medals
  • Sponsors
    • Sponsors
    • Suporting players
  • History
    • History

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

  • Functional Requirements
    • Show statistics for each member
    • Links to current sponsors
    • Link to FSU.no where the general statistics are.
  • Non-Functional Requirements
    • Add statistics to the site as easily as possible
    • Sponsors should be listed on the main page
    • All contact information should be accessible within 1 click on the page
